"Ancient Runes:
The language of Ancient Runes predates written history in “The Chronomancer’s Cipher.”  These cryptic symbols channel arcane energies, enabling spellcasters to manipulate time’s flow. Scholars debate their orig ins—some claim they’re remnants of the First Aeon, others that the runes shift meaning depending on the reader’s destiny and intent.

Griffin of Sky Peaks
The Griffin, a majestic creature, soars above the wind-carved Sky Peaks, its feathers shimmering with traces of latent magic. Fiercely territrial, it guards ancient secrets and lost artifacts. Many seek its wisdom, but few earn its trust; legends say a single feather grants visions of possible futures.
